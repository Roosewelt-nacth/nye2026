Welcome to the official repository for the Victorians Youth Group New Year's Eve Act. This project is a mobile-responsive, interactive web experience designed for an audience to engage with during a live performance.

During our New Year's Eve act, the audience is invited to scan a QR code that transports them to a "Digital Telegraph." As we transition from 2025 into 2026, the website serves as a medium for:

The Final Farewell: A dramatic goodbye to the past year.

The 2026 Revelation: An immersive, animated welcome to the New Year.

Personalized Blessings: A random Bible verse generator (100+ verses) to provide hope and guidance for every individual in the audience.
